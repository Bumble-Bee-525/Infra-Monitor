What is Infra-Monitor?

The Task

 Infra-Monitor‚Äôs purpose is to monitor, report, and display infrastructure issues nationwide by building an extensive database. The United States has some of the most expansive infrastructure on earth, with over 4 million miles of roads, 600,000 bridges, and at least 330,000 traffic lights according to the Federal Highway Administration. However, much of this infrastructure is aged and needs constant inspection. But how can everything built be continuously tracked and maintained? Infra-Monitor solves this by turning the general public into a network of inspectors without formal training.

Server Side

 The public can use the website to report an infrastructure issue they encounter and send the location. Then, this is used to build the database on the back end or server side, which can be used by the Department of Transportation and Federal Highway Administration so they can find where problems are and fix them promptly.

Client Side

 On the front-end, Infra-Monitor benefits the users by displaying all the potholes, sidewalk damage, street light issues, traffic light malfunctions, obstructed roads, and street drainage problems that people reported in the user‚Äôs area. Users can also see how many people have verified any particular entry to see which ones are the most problematic. This way, Infra-Monitor will also allow people to embark on journeys with prior knowledge of problems that may not be visible on most navigation apps like Google Maps, and can encourage people who live in areas with relatively poor infrastructure to persuade their local government to fix issues.

Usability

 The use of Infra-Monitor is also exceptionally straightforward. For people who may be driving or biking, Infra-Monitor can use browsers‚Äô built in AI speech recognition to listen for commands to report on infrastructure by voice. Additionally, the app is built to run inside of web browsers as a web app so that any machine, whether it be Android, IOS, Amazon FireOS, PC, Mac, can run Infra-Monitor without having to deal with installing or updating the program. This prevents differences in operating systems from limiting the reach of Infra-Monitor. People can also use Infra-Monitor to automatically look up their local city services online so they can report problems directly.

The Result

 This app can even save lives by preventing fatal car accidents caused by potholes and other critical failures of infrastructure by spreading information and helping the government fix them. Ultimately this will lead to safer, efficient, and reliable infrastructure for the benefit of everyone.
